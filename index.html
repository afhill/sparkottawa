Spark Ottawa

A spark of insight can ignite something incredible.

Spark Ottawa (sparkottawa.slack.com) is a community to bring together Ottawa-based digital professionals to learn, grow and share with each other.

It doesn't matter whether you're a designer, a product manager, a developer or hold a different job title, you're welcome here in this community. Whether you work in the private or public sector, you're welcome here. Whether you're at company A or B, government department C or D, you're welcome.

We're in this creative field to improve products, services and peoples' lives; let's help each other among the way.
